Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do your real estate services cover?

Our real estate services cover residential and commercial properties throughout Nigeria, with a primary focus on Ibadan and surrounding areas. We offer property development, sales, rentals, and management services tailored to each client's specific needs.

How do I engage your interior decoration services?

To engage our interior decoration services, simply contact us through our website, email, or phone. We'll schedule a consultation to discuss your vision, style preferences, and budget.

Our team will then prepare a detailed proposal with design concepts, timeline, and cost estimates for your review and approval before we begin work.

What types of agricultural products do you specialize in?

We specialize in a variety of agricultural products including cash crops, vegetables, and fruits. Our farming operations incorporate modern agricultural techniques and sustainable practices to ensure high-quality yields while preserving the environment for future generations.

How do you ensure water quality in your water factory?

We ensure water quality through a rigorous multi-stage purification process that includes filtration, UV treatment, and ozonation. Our facilities are regularly inspected and certified by relevant health authorities, and we conduct daily quality tests to maintain our high standards.

What areas do you cover for cleaning and fumigation services?

Our cleaning and fumigation services cover residential homes, commercial buildings, offices, schools, hospitals, and industrial facilities primarily in Ibadan and surrounding areas. We use eco-friendly products and follow industry best practices to ensure effective results without compromising health and safety.
